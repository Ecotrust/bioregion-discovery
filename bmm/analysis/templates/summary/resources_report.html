Net Primary Production Learn More

According to Wikipedia, Net Primary Production (NPP) is the measure of organic compound production from atmospheric or aquatic carbon dioxide. It is the rate at which all plants in an ecosystem produce net useful chemical energy.

NPP is measured in mass / area / time. The following reports use a measure of grams of carbon per meter squared per year.
